Scientific Positioning

My work lies at the intersection of biology, mathematics, and computer science. I design and apply rigorous bioinformatics approaches for the analysis of complex omics data, with a clear objective: producing robust, interpretable, and meaningful results for biomedical, environmental, and ecological research.

Transcriptomics (RNA-seq & scRNA-seq)

Bulk and single-cell transcriptomic analyses including quality control, normalization, differential expression, clustering, functional annotation, and biological interpretation.

Microbiome & Metagenomics

16S / ITS analyses using QIIME2 and DADA2, including alpha/beta diversity, taxonomic profiling, multivariate statistics, and integration of environmental and clinical metadata.

Reproducible Pipelines

Design of reproducible bioinformatics pipelines integrating automation, dependency management, documentation, FAIR principles, and deployment on high-performance computing environments.

Data Science & Modeling

Advanced statistical analyses and machine learning methods applied to biological and environmental datasets for exploration, classification, and predictive modeling.

Visualization & Scientific Reporting

Publication-ready figures, interactive dashboards (R Shiny), reproducible reports, and clear data communication for research and decision-making.

Scientific Support & Mentoring

Experimental design assistance, methodological review, bioinformatics support, and tailored training for research projects.

Vision & Commitment

Bioinformatics is, to me, a scientific and technological lever in the service of life sciences.

My academic journey, from geosciences to bioinformatics, has led me to develop an integrative approach to biological and environmental data, combining quantitative rigor, biological insight, and advanced computational tools.

In the long term, my goal is to contribute to the emergence of a structured African bioinformatics ecosystem, connected to local challenges and capable of supporting informed decision-making in public health, sustainable agriculture, and ecology.

A rigorous and reproducible approach

  • Transcriptomics: RNA-seq and scRNA-seq analyses
  • Microbiome: diversity analysis and multi-data integration
  • Pipelines: automated and reproducible workflows
  • Visualization: interactive exploration and reporting
  • Modeling: data science applied to life sciences

These skills have been developed and applied in academic and applied research environments (INRAE, LBBE, CRCL), with a strong emphasis on reproducibility, result interpretability, and interdisciplinary collaboration. They now form the foundation of my long-term scientific commitment.
